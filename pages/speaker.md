Ali Ghorbani is a professional photographer, educator, and keynote speaker in Hong Kong who bridges art, technology, and business storytelling. With a career spanning 35+ countries, he has photographed Hollywood icons including Robert De Niro, Leonardo DiCaprio, and Mariah Carey, and worked with global leaders such as LVMH, HSBC, Porsche, Disney, and Four Seasons.

Beyond photography, Ali delivers corporate training workshops and executive creativity programs that help organizations unlock innovation and communicate with impact. Since 2017, he has designed and led sessions for Adobe, The British Consulate, Soho House, the Hong Kong Golf Club, and international schools. His corporate workshops in Hong Kong and Asia focus on visual storytelling, AI in content creation, and digital brand strategy, giving teams practical tools to lead with creativity in a fast-changing world.

As an art partner with AIA, Ali co-curated Hong Kong’s largest outdoor photography exhibition, catering to more than 600,000 visitors. He has lectured at the Chinese University of Hong Kong on NFT marketing strategies and spoken at Adobe’s “Explore the Digital Art Revolution” on the future of visual communication. 

Ali combines artistry, technology, and business insight to inspire leaders and teams. His keynote speeches and corporate workshops are designed to spark creativity, strengthen brand trust, and help organizations thrive in the digital-first era.
